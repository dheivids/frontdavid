PARQUE NACIONAL DE YOSEMITE

Not just a great valley, but a shrine to human foresight, the strength of granite, the power of glaciers, the persistence of life, and the tranquility of the High Sierra. First protected in1864 , Yosemite National Park is best known for its waterfalls, but within its nearly 1,200 square miles, you can find deep valleys, grand meadows, ancient giant sequoias, a vast wilderness area, and much more.

Camping is extremely popular in Yosemite. Plan ahead and figure out what options you have for this enjoyable activity.
